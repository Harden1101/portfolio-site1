Hello, I'm Mitchell Harden

I'm a freelance interactive designer based in Wichita, Kansas, with a rich background in graphic design spanning over ten years. My true passion lies in storytelling through dynamic and engaging user experiences, particularly in the realms of websites and mobile apps.

In my career, I've traversed traditional graphic design and print, but my heart beats fastest when making things move and creating excitement through innovative design. What sets me apart is my commitment to transforming the digital landscape. I aim to inject more interactivity into online platforms, crafting experiences that not only captivate but entice users to revisit.

Join me on this creative journey, where each click, swipe, and scroll contributes to a compelling narrative. Let's collaborate to design an internet that goes beyond the ordinary, fostering meaningful connections between users and the immersive digital worlds we create. Welcome to a space where design meets interactivity, and every pixel has a purpose.
